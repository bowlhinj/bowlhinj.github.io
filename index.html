BOWLHINJ - AÇAI SHOP

is your local stop for refreshing açaí bowls. We use healthy frozen fruit and chia pudding to give you the perfect mix of taste and nutrition. All in one bowl.

AÇAÍ FILLING

  • Blended smoothie made from acai powder, frozen berries, coconut joghurt, some fruit and sugar
  • The smoothie is then cooled, making it more refreshing and helping the flavors meld together

CHIA PUDDING

  • healthy bowl base made out of chia seeds, joghurt and honey
  • left over night the chia seeds absorb the liquid and form a thick, pudding like texture

Ingredients

We use healthy ingrefients to make out bowls. Bowls are made at the moment of order, to ensure freshness and quality. We use frozen fruit and chia pudding to make out bowls the best they can be.

Açaí berries

Açaí is a small purple berry from the Amazon rainforest, known for its powerful antioxidants and health benefits. It has a delicious flavor and is the base of our bowls.
